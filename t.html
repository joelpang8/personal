Introduction

Joel Pang

Hi! I'm Joel Pang, a rising senior at the Rudolf Steiner School. Ever since my freshman year, I have always admired the streetwear scene. From the rise of Off-White to the dominance of Supreme in streetwear culture, I have found it all very fascinating. A huge part of this culture that got me interested was reselling. As a high school student, there aren't too many opportunities to make money, and reselling was the answer. I was fortunate to start off my reselling journey successfully, starting with Supreme merchandise. As I progressed and became more familiar with streetwear, I started to resell an array of streetwear products. StockX became popular at the time, and it was one of the apps that helped me learn how to resell more efficiently. To this day, I constantly check StockX to see the newest asks and bids on certain products, which helps me understand the market more.Â
